What does MuseLove do?

MuseLove is a company meant to put out up and coming artists into the world and support them in reachin there goals of becoming a musician. We mainly focus on raising money by selling pianos from other companys and using the money to support small musicans. Buying from us not only helps our company but also helps anyone wanting to become something but just doesn't have the funds to.
